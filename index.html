Quick Start to Using Gemini at DI

This interactive guide will help you get started and make the most of your AI assistant. Learn how to ask questions, generate content, write code, and more. Use the navigation to explore different topics.

Starting a Conversation

The simplest way to interact with Gemini is to type your request directly into the chatbox. You can ask questions, give commands, or provide prompts for Gemini to work with.

"Write a short story about a talking cat."

Asking Questions & Getting Information

Direct Questions & Explanations

Ask factual questions or request explanations on complex topics. You can also ask for simplified versions or analogies.

"Explain quantum physics in simple terms."

Summaries

Provide text and ask Gemini to summarize it for you, extracting the key points.

Creative Writing & Brainstorming

Gemini can help you draft emails, stories, poems, and more. Specify the tone, length, and style you prefer for the best results.

"Draft a formal email to a client about a project delay."

Subject: Update on Project [Project Name]

Dear [Client Name],

I am writing to provide an update on the [Project Name]. We have encountered an unforeseen circumstance that will cause a slight delay in our original timeline...

Coding Assistance

Describe the functionality you need, and Gemini can generate code snippets, explain code, help debug, or refactor existing code.

"Create a simple HTML page with a navigation bar and a hero section."

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>My Page</title>
</head>
<body>
  <nav>...</nav>
  <section class="hero">...</section>
</body>
</html>

Editing & Refining

If you want to change something in Gemini's response, simply tell it what you'd like to modify. The more specific you are, the better the result.

"Make that story longer and add a plot twist."

Using the Immersive Canvas

When Gemini provides a longer response, like code or detailed text, it will often appear in the "Immersive Canvas" on the right side of your screen. This is a collaborative space where you can directly edit the content. For code, you can use the "Preview" button to see the live output.

Tips for Best Results

Be Clear and Specific

The more precise your instructions, the better Gemini can understand and fulfill your request. Include details like length, tone, and format.

Break Down Complex Tasks

For very large or intricate requests, consider breaking them into smaller, more manageable steps for Gemini to handle.

Provide Context

If your request builds on previous interactions, remind Gemini of the context if necessary to ensure continuity.

Experiment!

Don't hesitate to try different types of prompts and see what Gemini can do. Exploration is key to understanding its full potential.
